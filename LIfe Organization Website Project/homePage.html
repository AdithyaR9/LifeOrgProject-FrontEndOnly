Finances

To-Do List

Never lose track of all the important errands you have to do, or small tasks you have to complete. This will make sure you never forget to do something, and prevents procrastination.

Health

Live healthy. Keep track of your daily supplements and medicins. Keep youself in peak physical condition and know the physiological state of your body. Log data such as your height, weight, and heartrate.
